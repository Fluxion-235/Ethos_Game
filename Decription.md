Game Data Report: Precision1. Game OverviewGame Name: PrecisionGame Type: Team-Based, Objective-Oriented Multiplayer ArenaObjective: "Precision" is a "Capture the Gem" style game. Two teams, Team A (Red) and Team B (Blue), compete to steal the opposing team's gem and return it to their own base to score a point.Winning Conditions: The first team to score 3 points wins the match. If neither team reaches 3 points, the team with the higher score at the end of the 12-minute match timer is declared the winner.2.
